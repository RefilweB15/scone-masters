Our Scone Recipes

Classic Plain Scone

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Cut in butter until crumbly.
  4. Add milk and mix into dough.
  5. Roll, cut, and bake for 12–15 mins.

Currant or Raisin Scone

  1. Follow plain scone steps.
  2. Add ½ cup raisins or currants after butter step.
  3. Shape and bake as usual.

Cheese Scone

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and mustard powder.
  2. Cut in butter, then add cheese.
  3. Mix in milk, roll, cut, and bake.

Chocolate Chip Scone

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Cut in butter, add chocolate chips.
  3. Stir in milk, form dough.
  4. Roll out, cut, bake at 220°C.

Blueberry Scones

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in cold butter, then gently fold in fresh blueberries.
  3. Add milk to form a soft dough; shape and cut into wedges.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15–18 minutes until golden brown.

Strawberry Scones

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in cold butter, then fold in chopped fresh strawberries.
  3. Add cream or milk to bring the dough together; shape and cut.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15–18 minutes until golden and firm.

Carrot Scones

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and a pinch of cinnamon.
  2. Cut in cold butter, then fold in grated carrots and optional raisins.
  3. Stir in milk to form a dough; shape and cut into rounds or wedges.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15–18 minutes until golden and cooked through.

Pineapple Scones

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in cold butter, then mix in drained crushed pineapple.
  3. Add a little pineapple juice or milk to form a soft dough; shape and cut.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15–18 minutes until golden and lightly crisp.

Ginger Scones

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground ginger in a bowl.
  2. Cut in cold butter, then stir in finely chopped crystallized ginger.
  3. Add milk or cream to form a dough; shape and cut into rounds or wedges.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15–18 minutes until golden and fragrant.

Vanilla Scones

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Cut in cold butter until crumbly, then stir in vanilla extract.
  3. Add milk or cream to form a soft dough; shape and cut as desired.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15–18 minutes until golden and slightly firm.
